How To Play

  • Use Arrow Keys on keyboard, or swipe/drag on the board.
  • Every move slides all tiles in one direction.
  • When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one bigger tile.
  • Reach the 2048 tile to win, then press Keep Going to continue.
  • The game ends when the board is full and no merge is possible.

Quick demo: start by combining 2 + 2 = 4, then 4 + 4 = 8, and keep doubling while keeping one corner organized.
